# Historical maps: Irving Park Rd & Paulina St, Chicago

Historical maps and aerial imagery covering the intersection of W Irving Park Rd and N Paulina St in Chicago's Lakeview community area (about 4000 N / 1700 W).

- **Input:** "Irving Park Rd and Paulina Ave in Chicago" (intersection)
- **Decimal (WGS84):** 41.954221, -87.671456
- **EPSG:3435:** 1164286, 1926603
- **Retrieved:** 2026-07-08
